# Building in Public

Most of what I build stays private for too long. This is an attempt to fix that.

## Why build in public

The act of writing about something forces clarity. You can't write clearly about something you don't understand.

Shipping things early — even imperfect things — creates feedback loops that are hard to get otherwise.

## The tradeoff

Public building has a cost. It requires actually finishing things, or at least reaching some state that's worth showing.

But that constraint is mostly a good one.

## What I'm working on

Mostly small tools, experiments, and things I need that don't exist yet. More on that as things take shape.
