stills / the terminal

Five crops from one clean frame

All from 32 seconds, native resolution. Useful as the reduced-motion fallbacks, and for anywhere a loop would be too much.

The chart. Pair, leverage, mark and oracle price, 24h change, volume, open interest and funding, in one strip.
The book. Both ladders and the spread.
The ticket. Cross margin, leverage, market or limit, size, reduce-only, take profit and stop loss.
Reverse, frozen. The fallback when motion is off.
Five positions, live. Size, entry, mark, PNL with ROE, liquidation price, margin, and per-row limit / market / reverse controls.
